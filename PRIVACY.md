## Privacy Policy

Cambridge Audio Downloader does not collect, store, or transmit any user data.

The extension only processes audio elements from the currently active Cambridge Dictionary page locally within the user's browser.

No personal information, browsing history, or user activity is tracked or shared.

All functionality is performed entirely on the client side.
